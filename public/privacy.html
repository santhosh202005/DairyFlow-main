Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

Welcome to DairyFlow. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application and web portal.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using the DairyFlow application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with our dairy farm tracking services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, username, password, gender, and address.
  • Contact Details: Phone number (used strictly for account creation, login, and secure OTP authentication).
  • Dairy Operations Data: Milk delivery entries (liters, fat/rate, AM/PM shift, dates) and financial records (advances, deductions, cattle feed purchases) to calculate your bills.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to operate and improve the DairyFlow app, including:

  • Authenticating your login requests via username/password or secure OTP.
  • Tracking milk delivery history and calculating automatic billing.
  • Managing record logs for customer advances and cattle feed deductions.
  • Displaying statistics and dashboard data relevant to your dairy farm.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Your privacy is important to us. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data is only processed internally to perform calculations for your farm's records, except when sending SMS via our third-party provider (Fast2SMS) to deliver verification OTPs to your registered phone number.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and administrative security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

5. Your Rights and Access Control

You can view, modify, or update your personal account information directly within the app settings. If you wish to delete your account or have any data-related queries, please contact your dairy farm administrator.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact your DairyFlow administrator.
